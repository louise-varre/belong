The Drinks

Join us for a drink with our neighbours on the second floor of the Conary, following our talk.

There is a particular pleasure in recognising your own values in someone else's work, especially when you were not looking for it. Five brands, working in different materials and from different countries, found ourselves sharing a floor at 3 Days of Design and discovered we had more in common than we expected: a quiet honesty in materials, hands that stay close to the work, and objects made for the moments that matter rather than the moments that perform.

Libeco Home (BE), HEGE (JP), soca (JP), Stylescape (NO) and Eldvarm. Come and meet the designers, the teams, and the people sharing our floor.

Please join us.
